Diploma Course

A diploma course in computers is a six months to one-year program designed to provide practical and technical knowledge in areas such as software development, networking, hardware, and database management. It is ideal for students who want to gain job-oriented skills quickly and enter the IT industry.





University Course

A university course in computers, such as BCA or B.Sc. Computer Science, is a three-year program that focuses on both theoretical and practical aspects of computing, including programming, algorithms, data structures, and system design. It provides a strong foundation for higher studies and technical careers.

Training Program

A training course is a short-term program, typically lasting 45 days, that focuses on specialized topics like Python, Java, Web Development, AI, or Cybersecurity. These courses are designed for quick skill enhancement and are often taken by professionals or students looking to upgrade their knowledge.
