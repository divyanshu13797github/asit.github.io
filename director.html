Who We Are

ASIT EduTech has been a leading institution in vocational training, skill development and Web/Application development for many years, renowned for its exceptional training programs. Established in July 2003 under the Minority Social Service Welfare Committee (Registered 21-1860, Registration No: 1792), the institute is committed to providing high-quality IT training.

We offer comprehensive courses in front-end and back-end development, covering essential technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Angular, Node.js, PHP, and Python.
