What is Training Program?

At ASIT Institute, we offer a wide range of training programs to help students and professionals enhance their technical skills and gain hands-on experience. Our training programs include Summer Training, Winter Training, Vocational Training, Industrial Training, Apprenticeship Training, Internship Training, Project Training, and Syllabus Training. Each program is carefully designed to meet the needs of students, freshers, and professionals, covering the latest technologies such as C, C++, Java, Python, Android Development, Web Development, AI, ML, Cloud Computing, and more. Our training sessions include practical learning, real-time projects, and industry exposure to ensure skill development. We provide certificates upon successful completion of each program, making students job-ready with in-depth technical knowledge and hands-on experience. Our Training Program





Summer Training

Summer training is a short-term program conducted during summer vacations, designed to help students enhance their technical skills. It includes in-depth learning of various programming languages and technologies like C, C++, Java, Python, Android Development, Web Development, and more. Students will also work on real-time projects to gain practical experience. Certificate is provided after successful completion.

Vocational Training

Vocational training focuses on industry-oriented skill development to prepare students for specific job roles. This training covers software development, networking, cybersecurity, database management, and other domains. Affordable packages are available. Industry-recognized certificate is awarded.

Winter Training

Winter training is conducted during winter vacations and offers a condensed yet powerful learning experience. This training also includes hands-on projects. Course-based pricing applies. Certificate is provided after course completion.

Industrial Training

ndustrial training is an advanced-level program designed for students who want to gain real-world experience in the IT industry. This training includes working on live projects under expert guidance, focusing on industrial coding practices, frameworks, and development methodologies. Fee depends on project complexity. Certificate is provided with project completion proof.




Apprenticeship Training

Apprenticeship training is a structured program that combines classroom instruction with hands-on work experience. Students get a chance to work in real industrial environments and understand the workflow of tech companies. Fees may vary, and some companies offer stipends. Certificate is provided along with work experience proof.

Syllabus Training

Syllabus training is specially designed to help students cover their academic syllabus effectively. This includes programming subjects, database management, operating systems, data structures, and other technical subjects. It ensures conceptual clarity and practical implementation. Subject-based pricing applies. Certificate is provided on course completion.
