ASIT Institute: A Prestigious Vocational Training Center

ASIT Institute has been playing a leading role in skill development and vocational training for more than 22 years and has established itself as a trusted centre of excellence. It was founded in July 2003 under the Alpsankhyak Samaj Sewa Kalyan Samiti (Registration 21-1860, Registration Number: 1792). The institute provides high-quality IT training programs to help and proper guidance to students for developing technical skills and better career opportunities in government and private sectors.

Foundation of Success

The success of ASIT Institute is based on the dedication, hard work, and commitment of its skilled trainers and staff members. The institute promotes an interactive learning environment, ensuring effective communication between students and trainers. This helps students overcome academic and professional challenges and development of their overall personality.

Affiliations & Recognitions

ASIT Institute is affiliated with several reputed government-recognized institutions, including:

  • National Institute of Electronics & Information Technology (NIELIT), New Delhi (Code: 9907510)
  • Rajiv Gandhi Computer Saksharta Mission (RGCSM), Kota, Rajasthan (Code: UP-368)
  • Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay Education Group (DDEG), Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh (Code: AI16DDEG)
  • Swami Vivekanand Subharti University, Meerut, Uttar Pradesh (Code: C5/6)

Our Mission & Vision

The main objective of ASIT Institute is to provide vocational education as per industrial requirements. It helps students to attain technical expertise, professional skills development. The institute is committed to preparation of skilled professionals to secure their future in government and private sectors or establish their own businesses.

Comprehensive Training Programs

Apart from IT and technical training, the institute offers special vocational courses for both men and women, including:

  • Tailoring & Embroidery Training
  • Beautician & Fashion Designing Course
  • English & Hindi Typing Training
  • Stenography (Short-Hand) Training
  • Mobile & Computer Repairing Training
  • English Speaking & Personality Development Training
  • Career Guidance & Professional Counselling

A Legacy of Excellence

Over the years, thousands of ASIT Institute's students have successfully secured jobs in various government and private organizations. Their expertise, knowledge, and professional efficiency have helped to enhance the institute reputation at the national and international platforms.

ASIT Institute remains committed to delivering quality education and skill development. It continues to train future professionals and equip them with the necessary resources to succeed in an ever-changing, challenging, and competitive fields.
